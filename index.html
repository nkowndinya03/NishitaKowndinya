About Me

Hi, and thank you for visiting my portfolio!
I am Nishita Kowndinya, a committed psychology student who is enthusiastic about learning about mental health, human behaviour, and the complex inner workings of the mind. As I go towards a Master of Science in Psychology at Sister Nivedita University, I hope to make a significant contribution to the field by fusing my academic background with real-world experience. My knowledge of psychology's real-world applications has grown as a result of these experiences, which have also improved my abilities in active listening, effective communication, counselling, leadership, analysis, and research, among other areas.
